## Summary
NVIDIA Parabricks is the Clara genomics software solution for GPU-accelerated next-generation sequencing workflows. Current Clara docs describe Parabricks as supporting short- and long-read applications, workflows from basecalling through tertiary analysis, and GATK4 Best Practices-compatible pipelines with major acceleration versus CPU-only BWA-GATK4 pipelines.

## Detail
Parabricks belongs under [[NVIDIA-Clara]] as the genomics acceleration component. It is distinct from [[NVIDIA-BioNeMo]], which focuses on biomolecular AI and drug discovery foundation models, though both live in NVIDIA's healthcare and life sciences portfolio.

The current Parabricks documentation covers overview/release notes, getting started, hardware and software requirements, cloud usage guides, DeepVariant training, tool reference material, publications, and compatibility with baseline tools.
