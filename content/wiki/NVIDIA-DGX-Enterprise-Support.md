## Summary
NVIDIA DGX Enterprise Support and Services is NVIDIA's support, infrastructure-services, and education-services program for the DGX platform. It helps enterprises keep DGX systems, DGX BasePOD, and DGX SuperPOD deployments operating at production standards through support cases, remote hardware and software support, deployment guidance, onboarding, optimization, training, and optional add-on services.

## Detail

### Purpose
DGX infrastructure is not just hardware; it is a production AI factory platform with GPU systems, networking, storage, operating systems, drivers, orchestration, and workload software. DGX Enterprise Support provides the human support and services layer around that platform so organizations can deploy, operate, troubleshoot, optimize, and renew DGX environments with NVIDIA expertise.

### Support and services scope
- Included enterprise-grade support with 24/7 case intake, remote hardware and software support, onboard diagnostic support, and NVIDIA technical experts.
- Add-on options such as business-critical support, onsite engineers for field-replaceable units, technical account manager support, media retention, onsite spares, site reliability engineering, and coordinated support.
- Infrastructure Specialist services for DGX installation, deployment, custom workshops, DGX orientation, and BasePOD/SuperPOD onboarding.
- Education services and courses covering DGX systems administration, DGX BasePOD administration, DGX SuperPOD administration, and AI infrastructure and operations.
