## Summary
NVIDIA Silicon Photonics is NVIDIA's optical networking direction for scaling AI factory fabrics with lower power and higher density. Current NVIDIA pages connect silicon photonics to next-generation Spectrum-X Ethernet, ConnectX-9, Vera Rubin, and rack-scale AI infrastructure.

## Detail

### Purpose
AI factories increasingly need high-bandwidth optical links between GPU racks, storage systems, and network switches. Silicon photonics integrates optical communication closer to networking silicon, reducing power and increasing density compared with traditional pluggable optics at large scale.

### Current public positioning
- Optical networking technology for next-generation AI data centers.
- Connected to Spectrum-X Ethernet, high-speed AI fabrics, and Vera Rubin platform direction.
- Relevant to 1.6 Tb/s-class networking and large NVL/rack-scale systems.
- Complements [[NVIDIA-ConnectX-9]], Spectrum switches, and InfiniBand/Ethernet AI networking.
